Safe Abortion Resources

Nearly 1 in 4 women will have an abortion by the age 45 in America.

On Friday June 24th, 2022, the Supreme Court overturned Roe v. Wade. By the court justices ruling in favor of the overturning, each state now has the power to determine what restrictions they will place on abortions, giving lawmakers the power to make medical decisions for us instead of making them ourselves.

Doing this strips all birthing people of their constitutional right to have an abortion and also endangers our lives by sending the message that no one’s bodily autonomy is respected or valued in this country. With many states supporting this ruling and moving to ban abortions, BIPOC and marginalized communities will be the most harmed.

Now more than ever, we as a collective need to stay informed, educated, and open! To do this, you need to help spread information and advocate for change. The overturning of Roe v. Wade will not stop abortions. It will only prevent SAFE abortions. Below you will be able to find many different resources to learn more about these law changes that may affect you, as well as more resources for safe abortions.

Stay safe and stay informed! :)
