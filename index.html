As Our Mission

mariposa is a site aiming to raise awareness of multiple areas of human rights by providing a network of resources. As of now, we hope for our site to help provide resources regarding immigration, safe abortions, and gun laws. Another one of our goals for the website is to shed light on gender equality, minorities, and LGBTQ+ rights. We hope our website will help you become more informed and a better advocate for change!
