LGBTQ+ Resources

21% of Gen-Z adults identify as LGBTQ+.

Discrimination goes beyond race, LGBTQ+ are often discriminated against because of their identity. Studies show that 30% of LGBTQ+ adults have experienced discrimination/exclusion from financial services. These actions grow the wealth disparity between the LGBTQ+ community to their straight cis counterparts. With 27 states having no laws that explicitly protect discrimination against a persons sexual orientation and gender identity, it makes it difficult to hold those who discriminate against LGBTQ+ individuals accountable.

People apart of the LGBTQ+ community have been fighting for their basic rights for years. While some progress has been made it is imperdenant that we continue to push for more change until everyone is represented and treated equally and represented evenly. With this community comprising of people of all ages, races, and gender identities, something that we all need to remember is that one's gender identity and/or sexual/affectional orientation, is not one's choice it is who they are.
